How to deposit money from Mpesa to your GlobalGrowth account

  1. Using your phone, go to the Safaricom SIM tool kit menu.
  2. Click on M-Pesa.
  3. Tap on "Lipa na M-PESA" then the "PayBill" option.
  4. Tap "Enter business no."
  5. Key in the PayBill number 247247 as the business number for GlobalGrowth Investment.
  6. Click on "Account number".
  7. Key in the GlobalGrowth Investment Account Number: 0701905540
  8. Enter the Amount you wish to spend for the package.
  9. Key in your M-pesa Pin.
  10. A confirmation SMS from M-pesa and Equity Account "Global Growth Investment" will be sent to your safaricom line.
  11. Copy the M-pesa message, go to your Global Growth Investment Account Profile and paste the Mpesa Message for Admin approval. Refresh your Account in one minute time and confirm your deposit on the deposit balance section.
